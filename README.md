# **<h2 text-align="center">Attendance Management System</h2>**
<b>Attendance management system helps to maintain record and display attendance of students subjectwise and datewise.</b>
<b>This portal will help student as well as teacher to get updated with their attendance record and they can download them too! </b>
# **Take Attendance**
First they have to login through their ID and passwords. Students can see their attendence record and Teacher selects their particular subject and records the attendance datewise.
